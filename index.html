About Me

Hello! I'm David Gonzalez, a Front End Developer with a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating captivating user experiences. With a background in Industrial Engineering and over 10 years of experience in the manufacturing industry, I've recently taken a turn in my professional path to pursue my love for Web Development. As a Certified Scrum Developer, I bring my expertise in agile project management and collaborative development methodologies to every web project I work on. My experience in managing a manufacturing Product Development facility has honed my skills in planning, organizing, and optimizing processes, making me well-equipped to deliver high-quality code that's clean, efficient, and scalable. I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other modern web technologies, and I'm constantly staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends and best practices. I take pride in creating pixel-perfect designs and seamless user interactions that engage and delight users. When I'm not coding, you can find me indulging in my love for music and vide games. I'm excited to combine my engineering expertise, Scrum certification, and front-end development skills to create innovative web experiences that make a meaningful impact. Let's connect and discuss how I can bring your web projects to life with my unique blend of skills and experiences!
